F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some common questions regarding the SkillMatch platform.

What is SkillMatch?

SkillMatch is a platform that connects students and faculty within a campus based on their skills and project needs, enabling easy collaboration.

How do I create a profile?

To create a profile, simply sign up with your email, upload a profile picture, and specify your skills and interests.

Can I collaborate with others?

Yes! You can toggle your availability status to indicate whether you are open to collaboration and connect with others based on shared interests.

Is SkillMatch available on mobile?

Yes, SkillMatch is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile devices. You can access it anytime, anywhere!

How do I contact support?

If you have any issues or inquiries, you can contact our support team via email or the help section on the platform.

Are there any premium features?

At the moment, SkillMatch offers all features for free, but we are planning to introduce premium features in the future.
